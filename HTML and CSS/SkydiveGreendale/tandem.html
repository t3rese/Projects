Are you ready for the adventure of a lifetime?


Tandem skydiving is the safest training method for first-time jumpers. Whether your goal is to become a certified skydiver or you just want to cross it off your bucket list - a tandem is a great introduction to the sport. No ground school is required for a tandem jump. Students have to watch a short video and go through a briefing with an instructor, and you will be ready to jump! After a 50 second free-fall from 14,000 feet, you'll enjoy a scenic five-minute parachute flight before landing. Visit our Book Now page to make your reservation.
